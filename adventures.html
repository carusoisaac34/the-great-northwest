Adventures

Here at GNW, we want to offer our customers amazing opportunities to explore the Pacific Northwest through our partners at TheClymb.com. From backpacking in the Cascades to windsurfing in the Hood River, no visit to the Pacific Northwest would be complete without one of our signature vacation packages. We have partnered with TheClymb.com because they offer top of the line packages for the most inexpensive prices possible. Both of our missions align due to the fact that we both believe in supporting our employees with a quality lifestyle through an increased minimum wage of $25.00 each hour. We hire refugees from the heart of Africa and bring them to America to help them escape hostile living conditions and to give them quality employment.

Because all of our products are made by hand, we encourage our employees to work their hardest for the customer’s sake and we encourage them to take their time with everything they make.
